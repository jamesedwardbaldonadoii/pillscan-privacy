PillScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

PillScan collects the following information to provide our services:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Camera images (for pill identification — not stored permanently)
  • Scan history (stored securely in your account)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify pills using AI image analysis
  • To provide drug information from public databases
  • To save your scan history for future reference
  • To manage your subscription

3. Third Party Services

PillScan uses the following third party services:

  • Google Gemini AI — for pill image analysis
  • Supabase — for secure data storage
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management
  • OpenFDA — for drug information lookup

4. Camera Permission

PillScan requires camera access solely for pill identification. Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis and are not stored on our servers permanently.

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Supabase with Row Level Security to ensure your data is only accessible by you.

6. Data Retention

Your scan history is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen.

7. Children's Privacy

PillScan is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

8. Medical Disclaimer

PillScan is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
